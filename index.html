At AmberStudent, we're building the global platform students trust to find a home abroad — tools used by millions, decisions that affect real lives. We're hiring an AI Product Design Intern who doesn't think like a traditional designer.

06

Questions people ask

Why a pull request instead of a form?

Because we want designers who are comfortable in the tools builders use. If GitHub feels foreign today, learning it is the first thing we'd ask you to do anyway.

Do I need to code?

No. But you should be unafraid to read code, prompt a model to write it, and ship the result.

Can I apply if I'm not in Pune?

The role is 5 days onsite in Pune. If you're willing to relocate, say so in your PR.

What if I don't have a "portfolio"?

Send whatever shows how you think — a Notion page, a tweet thread, a Loom, a Figma file, a repo. We care about thinking, not polish.
