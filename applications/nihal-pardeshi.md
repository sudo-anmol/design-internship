## 4. A project you've shipped recently

https://khaoozy.tech

Khaoozy is a campus food pre-ordering app I built from zero at Vishwakarma
University. Students skip the queue by ordering in advance; vendors get
real-time order streams instead of rush-hour chaos. I owned the whole thing —
product scope, Next.js frontend, FastAPI backend, UPI payments, full order
lifecycle (pending → paid → approved → ready → collected), and a live canteen
staff dashboard. Hit 30+ orders in week one.

What I'd change: the staff dashboard was built last, under deadline, and it
shows. Vendors are the ones who actually live in it — accepting orders,
managing queues, marking ready — but I designed it from a student's POV. I'd
redo that side from scratch, starting with 30 minutes watching someone run the
counter.
